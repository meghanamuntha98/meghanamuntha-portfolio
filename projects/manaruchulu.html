Google Ads Case Study

Google Search Ads Campaign

Driving Qualified Traffic for an Authentic Andhra Pickles Brand

As part of my digital marketing internship at Manaruchulu, I planned, launched and optimized a Google Search campaign to improve brand visibility and attract users actively searching for authentic Andhra pickles. The campaign focused on keyword research, campaign structure, Responsive Search Ads, search term analysis and continuous optimization to drive high-quality website traffic.

Overview

Project Overview

Campaign Goal

The objective of this Google Search campaign was to increase website visibility and attract users actively searching for authentic Andhra pickles. The campaign focused on driving qualified traffic through relevant keywords, structured ad groups and continuous campaign optimization.

Business Challenge

Manaruchulu offers authentic homemade Andhra pickles through its online store. The challenge was to reach high-intent users while reducing irrelevant searches that could waste advertising budget.

Strategy

Campaign Objectives & Strategy

The campaign was designed to reach users actively searching for authentic Andhra pickles while minimizing irrelevant traffic. Instead of targeting broad audiences, the strategy focused on high-intent keywords, organized ad groups and continuous optimization to improve traffic quality.

Increase Qualified Traffic

Campaign Setup

Campaign Structure

Instead of placing every keyword into a single ad group, I organized the campaign based on customer search intent. This allowed each ad group to display more relevant Responsive Search Ads and direct users to the most appropriate landing page.

Veg Pickles

Targeted users searching for traditional vegetarian Andhra pickles including mango, gongura and homemade varieties.

Non-Veg Pickles

Focused on high-intent searches related to chicken, fish, prawn and mutton pickles.

Brand Searches

Captured users already searching for the Manaruchulu brand and directed them to the appropriate landing page.

Figure 1. Campaign structure showing separate ad groups for Veg Pickles, Non-Veg Pickles and Brand keywords. Organizing the campaign into intent-based ad groups improved ad relevance and enabled better optimization.

User Experience

Landing Page Strategy

Rather than directing every visitor to the homepage, I mapped each ad group to the most relevant landing page. This ensured that users reached content matching their search intent, creating a more relevant browsing experience and improving the overall campaign structure.

Veg Pickles

Users searching for vegetarian Andhra pickles were directed to the dedicated Veg Pickles category page, allowing them to browse relevant products immediately.

Non-Veg Pickles

Searches related to chicken, fish and prawn pickles were routed directly to the Non-Veg Pickles category, reducing unnecessary navigation.

Brand Searches

Users searching specifically for Manaruchulu were directed to the homepage where they could explore all product categories and featured collections.

Negative Keyword Strategy

Expand Negative Keywords

Strengthen Conversion Tracking
